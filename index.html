Survey Field Guide

Country

The INTERPOL Member State being surveyed

NCB Name

National Central Bureau - INTERPOL's contact point in each country (e.g., NCB Wiesbaden)

NCB Address

Contact information including postal address, email, and telephone number for the local NCB

Red Notice Arrest

Whether arrests can be made solely on the basis of a Red Notice. Some countries require additional legal proceedings; others (e.g., Germany) will arrest immediately

Diffusion Arrest

Similar to Red Notices - whether arrests can be made on Diffusion notices alone

Legal Basis

The applicable laws and regulations that authorize arrests based on INTERPOL notices in your country

System Used

Whether your country uses INTERPOL's I24/7 system, own local systems, or both

⚠️ Additional Capabilities

Notice Exclusion: Can your NCB exclude notices from national systems on a prima facie basis?

Access Requests: Can your national law allow requesting information about the existence of notices in INTERPOL systems?

Warning Obligation: In some countries, police must inform a wanted person if they obtain knowledge of an abusive Red Notice that could expose someone to arrest, torture, or ill-treatment upon leaving the country
